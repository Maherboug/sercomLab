Optical Communications

The Optical Communications team is a diverse group of experts from different backgrounds and institutions, all focused on advancing optical communications technology through collaborative research. With expertise in photonics, signal processing, fiber optics, and networking, they are poised to revolutionize connectivity.

Sofien Mhatli

Associate Professor, ISIK

Sofien Mhatli is currently a Senior Researcher at the Ecole Polytechnique de Tunis, La Marsa, Tunisia, and a Telecommunications Engineer at the Ministry of National Defense, Tunis, Tunisia. His expertise is focused on designing and optimizing optical and microwave devices for applications in wireless telecom, sensing systems, and optical communication systems (SDH, WDM, PON, CO-OFDM, IM-DD...). His research interests include digital signal processing, algorithmic design (e.g., machine learning), and optical devices (e.g., MZM) for optical communications. The outcome of his research resulted in prestigious peer-reviewed journal papers and top conference presentations (e.g., OSA Optics Letters, OFC, IEEE Photonics Journal, ECOC).(Based on document published on 8 March 2017). Prof.Mhatli he is now international IT & Cloud Trainer who deliver training courses in different countries such as France, suisse, UAE, KSA, Morocco, Ivory Cost, Senegal, Congo, Egypt, Jordanie, Butswana, Zimbabwei Prof.Mhatli his research topic are in the filed of Communications & cybersecyurity in the cloud or onpremises infrastructure.

Marwa Sammouda
