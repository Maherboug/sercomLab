Optical Communications

The Optical Communications (OC) team is a diverse group of experts from different backgrounds and institutions, all focused on advancing optical communications technology through collaborative research. With expertise in photonics, signal processing, fiber optics, and networking, they are poised to revolutionize connectivity.

Faouzi Bahloul

Senior Researcher|Optical Communication Team Leader | Professor ENIT&EPT

Faouzi BAHLOUL has been a teacher at the National Engineering School of Tunis (ENIT) since 2002. His areas of research are the applications of new generation fibers in optical communications systems. His recent work focuses on innovative multiple fiber optic components and fiber lasers.

Sofien Mhatli

Associate Professor, ISIK

Sofien Mhatli is currently a Senior Researcher at the Ecole Polytechnique de Tunis, La Marsa, Tunisia, and a Telecommunications Engineer at the Ministry of National Defense, Tunis, Tunisia. His expertise is focused on designing and optimizing optical and microwave devices for applications in wireless telecom, sensing systems, and optical communication systems (SDH, WDM, PON, CO-OFDM, IM-DD...). His research interests include digital signal processing, algorithmic design (e.g., machine learning), and optical devices (e.g., MZM) for optical communications.
