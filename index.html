About SERCOM

With an interdisciplinary approach in digital domains, the SERCOM laboratory was established in 2010 by researchers and professionals from various sectors. This initiative is dedicated to fostering cutting-edge research, thereby paving the path for exciting innovations and applications in the digital realm. SERCOM, under the auspices of the Ministry of Higher Education and Scientific Research, is affiliated with the Polytechnic School of Tunisia.

SERCOM's objectives encompass:

Contributing to the advancement of scientific research in IT and Telecommunications.

Proposing, supervising, and overseeing innovative research and development projects.

Collaborating with socio-economic partners both nationally and internationally.

Bridging the gap between scientific and academic research and the industrial sphere.

Facilitating the integration of students and recent graduates into the professional landscape.

Organizing scientific events on a national and international scale.

Research Themes

SERCOM's Research interests encompass:

Security and Scalable Systems

Image and Video Processing

Optical Communications

Embedded Systems

Digital Communications

Internet Of Things (IoT) and Big Data

Blockchain and Distributed systems

Artificial Intelligence and related Technologies
