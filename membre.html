Membres

Our team comprises multidisciplinary experts from various institutions and professional fields, each with relevant backgrounds in different research areas.

Rabah ATTIA

Directeur du laboratoire SERCOM | Professor | Ecole Polytechnique de Tunisie

Rabah Attia received the Ph.D. degree in telecommunications from the University of Valenciennes, France, in 1986. Since 2013, he has been the Director of the EPT/SERCOM Laboratory, Tunisia. He is currently a Professor of optical communication with the Polytechnic School of Tunisia. His research interests include new-generation of optical fiber, photonic crystal component, electro-optic modulators, and cooperative/relay networks.

DBLP: https://dblp.org/pid/60/7380.html

Takoua Abdellatif

Senior Researcher| Associate Professor

Takoua Abdellatif received the engineering degree in IT from the Engineering School, ENSIMAG, Grenoble, France, in 1998, and the Ph.D. degree in autonomic management in JavaEE clusters from INRIA, in 2006. She worked as a Research and Development Engineer on HP telecom platform with Hewlett Packard, Grenoble, for five years. Then, she was a Research Engineer with Bull/Grenoble on systems’ scalability and fault-tolerance. She is currently an Associate Professor in IT with the University of Sousse and the École Polytechnique de Tunisie (EPT), Carthage University. She is also a Senior Researcher with the EPT/SERCOM Research Laboratory, coordinating the laboratory research activities around scalable and secure distributed systems.

DBLP: https://dblp.org/pid/60/7380.html

Aymen Yahyaoui

Assistant Professor

Aymen Yahyaoui received the degree in computer science engineering from the Aviation School of Borj El Amri, in 2005, the M.S. degree in information system and network security from the High Institute of Computer Science, in 2007, the M.S. degree in modeling from the High Institute of Management, in 2009, and the M.S. degree in computer science from the Naval Postgraduate School, USA, in 2014. He is currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree with the Polytechnic School of Tunisia. He is also an Assistant Teacher with the Tunisian Military Academy of Fondouk Jedid. He works mainly on anomaly and intrusion detection in the IoT. He is also a member of the EPT/SERCOM Research Team.

Maher Boughdiri

Blockchain & IoT engineer | PhD Student

MAHER BOUGHDIRI earned his Telecommuni- cations Engineering degree from the Tunisian Mil- itary Academy in Tunisia, 2021. He also holds a Master of Advanced Engineering degree from the Tunisian Polytechnic School, in 2022. Currently, he is actively engaged in blockchain security re- search and is a dedicated member of the EPT/SER- COM Research Team. His research interests span various aspects of blockchain technology, with a primary focus on blockchain cybersecurity. He is committed to enhancing blockchain security and addressing challenges related to blockchain scalability and privacy. Maher’s research also involves exploring advanced techniques such as zero-knowledge proofs and homo- morphic encryption to bolster blockchain privacy and security.
